Boiled Potato

Soft and filling, boiled potatoes are a basic yet healthy side dish. Just boil, peel, and sprinkle salt for a quick and satisfying bite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large potato
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Boil potato until soft (about 15 minutes).
  2. Peel and cut into pieces.
  3. Sprinkle salt. Ready to eat!
