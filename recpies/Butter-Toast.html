Butter Toast

Crispy and buttery, this is one of the easiest snacks or breakfast options. Toasted bread with melted butter gives a warm, comforting taste.

Ingredients:

  • 2 bread slices
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps:

  1. Heat a pan.
  2. Spread butter on bread..
  3. Toast both sides until golden. Serve!
