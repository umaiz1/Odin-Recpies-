Fried Egg

A simple and quick dish, perfect for breakfast. Just crack an egg into a hot pan, cook, and enjoy a protein-rich meal within minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp oil or butter
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Crack the egg into the pan.
  3. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the white sets.
  4. Sprinkle salt. Done!
